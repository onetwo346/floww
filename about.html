About Floww

Pioneering the future of crypto brokerage with security, simplicity, and innovation

Our Story

Founded in 2025, Floww was born from a simple vision: to make cryptocurrency accessible to everyone, regardless of technical expertise or investment size.

What began as a small team of blockchain enthusiasts has grown into a global platform serving users across 150+ countries, processing over $1 billion in monthly transaction volume.

Our platform bridges the gap between traditional finance and the crypto economy, providing a secure, transparent, and user-friendly environment for individuals and businesses to participate in the digital asset revolution.
